Frequently Asked Questions

I just lost my pet, where do I start looking?
Don't panic! First, you should look at our found pets page to see if anybody has found your pet already! If not, we encourage you to submit a "lost pet report" and check whatever contact information you provide often!
I submitted a lost pet report. Is there anything else I can do in the meantime?
Oftentimes local animal control or animal shelters will pick up or receive lost pets. We encourage you to check both. Beyond that, if your pet has a vet tag your vet may be contacted as well. Remember to keep the contact information on your pet's collar or microchip up to date!
I found somebody's pet, how do I find his/her owner?
If the pet has a collar with contact information, start there! Otherwise, definitely check the lost pets page first to possibly find the owner. If not, we encourage you to submit a "found pet report" and you will hopefully be contacted by the pet's owner!
I found a pet, but it doesn't have a collar. Is this somebody's lost pet or a stray?
Many pets these days have a microchip. We encourage you to take the animal to a local vet or animal shelter in order to be scanned for one.
I found a lost pet, but I'm not sure if it's safe to approach. What should I do?
Your safety is our biggest priority. If you are ever hesitant to approach an animal, we advise you to call local animal control or your local police department.
I found a lost pet, but the owner hasn't responded to me. Where should I keep the pet?
Here at Finding Fido, we recommend that you take the pet to a local animal shelter if you can't reunite the pet with their owner right away. The shelter will be better equipped to house the pet until the owner is ready.
I found my pet through Finding Fido! How can I continue to show my support?
Our biggest joy is reuniting pets with their owners! We encouarge you to continue helping us with that endeavor by reporting any dogs that you may find, so as to help other owners just like you!
I haven't found any lost pets in my area. Are there other ways of showing my support?
We greatly appreciate any donations! You can donate to us through PayPal (note: donations are non-refundable, and any chargeback attempts will be contested)
