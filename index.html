About Us

What is Finding Fido?

Pets are many people’s best companions and family members. That’s why losing a pet can oftentimes be one of the most devastating things to people. We want to help these pet owners find their pets.

With this mission, Finding Fido is a platform where pet owners exchange information about their lost pets or pets they found. Here, you can report your lost pet or a pet you found. We also provide tips with pet searching for owners who just lost their beloved fluffy friends.
